Welcome to Dr. Virendra Pratap Sekhawat Class


What is it?
E-class is an innovative educational content platform for students in Maharashtra State Board schools (from 1st to 10th standards). It covers all subjects and is available in English, Marathi, Semi English, Hindi, and Urdu mediums, following the state syllabus.
Purpose:
E-class aims to provide high-quality digital content that aligns with the curriculum, making learning engaging and effective.
Features:
Whether you’re a student or a teacher, E-class offers resources that can enhance your educational experience. It’s a valuable tool for accessing subject-specific content, practice materials, and assessments.
